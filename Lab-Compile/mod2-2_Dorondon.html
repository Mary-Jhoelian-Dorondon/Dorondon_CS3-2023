Toilet Bound Hanako-Kun

Toilet Bound Hanako-Kun is an anime about the adventures of a female high school student and the ghost she accidentlly summoned in the girl's bathroom. Accompanied by their friends and magic tools, they take on adventures, spirits, and apparitions. In each episode, they take on new enemies and challenges and also thicken the lore of the anime, making us fans prone to making conspiracies. The two main characters are Yashiro Nene and Hanako-kun, Come now and watch Toilet Bound HAnako-Kun and join them in their adventures and maybe even their love story!
