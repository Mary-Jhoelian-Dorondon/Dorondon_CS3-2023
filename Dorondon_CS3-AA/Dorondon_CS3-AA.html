About The Website

What is this website about?
Image

This website is to help people who are passionate about manhwas and just want to have a good read. The manhwas will also be organized by genre. It will contain short reviews and parts of the manhwas plot which will help the user to have a grasp about the plot of the said manhwa. The website will also contain some panels of the manhwa and also some cover photos to show the artstyle.
