Action

  • Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint

    • This manhwa has a complex, slow-paced plot with several developments

    and foreshadowing. It’s an emotional rollercoaster which truly

    encapsulates life, as well as what it means to grieve and hope.

    The art style is IMMACULATE.

  • Weak Hero

    • This manhwa because it empowers the reader as a person.

    Everyone starts at the bottom as this seemingly weak minion,

    but eventually makes their way to the top as the level 100 OP boss.

    It taught me not to underestimate people, that’s for sure.

  • The Boxer

    • This manhwa taught me the hard way of things and the dark side

    of the world. It’s a tough read because you’ll be slapped across

    the face with the reality of boxing. Overall, it was great.

  • Eleceed

    • The plot is amazing because it’s a perfect Halo-Halo bowl of action,

    comedy, and cuteness overload. I also adore the art style and OMG,

    Jiyoung is so ATTRACTIVEEE.

  • Unholy Blood

    • This manhwa is one of my favorites because it has in-depth back

    stories of both the protagonists and the villains. The art style

    emphasizes its impact and the characters’ emotions. I also loved

    the balance between action, supernatural, comedy, family, as well

    as the romance between Yohan and Hayan.
