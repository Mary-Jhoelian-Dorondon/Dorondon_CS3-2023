REENA WYZ BETONIO - Batch 2027 Student Alliance Representative

A week before the family day celebration, our preparation kicked off with the guidance of our teachers. Ma'am Yeye, adviser of Potassium, got us started on the zumba segment by sharing a video as a dance reference. Immediately after receiving it, I gathered our batch together to start practicing and brainstorming choreography. Each afternoon, we dedicated our time to rehearsing in the covered court, where Ma'am Yeye generously lent her expertise in choreography. Simultaneously, for the singing competition, Ma'am Marianne, adviser of Lithium, took charge of coaching our batch's participants. Additionally, when it came to the sports activities, we ensured that every participant who signed up possessed some level of athleticism. In short, with our teachers' help, we practiced hard for the family day. We worked on zumba, singing, and sports, making sure everyone was ready. We definitely made the event incredibly enjoyable!
